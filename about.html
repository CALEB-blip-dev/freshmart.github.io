Our Story

Founded in 2025, FreshMart began as a small family-owned grocery store in central Kampala. What started as a neighborhood market has grown into Kampala's favorite online grocery delivery service.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, high-quality groceries at affordable prices with convenient home delivery across Kampala.
